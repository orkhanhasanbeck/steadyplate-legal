# Privacy Policy for SteadyPlate

**Last Updated: January 18, 2026**

## Overview

SteadyPlate ("the App") is a nutrition and health tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our App.

## Data Storage

**All your data is stored locally on your device.** SteadyPlate does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers.

Your data, including:
- Meal logs and nutrition information
- Weight entries
- Fasting records
- Exercise logs
- Goals and preferences

...remains entirely on your device and under your control.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal identification information
- We do not track your location
- We do not collect analytics or usage data
- We do not share any data with third parties
- We do not use cookies or tracking technologies

## Device Permissions

SteadyPlate requests the following permissions:

### Camera Access
- **Purpose:** To take photos of meals for AI-powered nutrition analysis
- **Usage:** Photos are processed to extract nutritional information and are stored locally on your device
- **Control:** You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in your device settings

### Motion & Fitness Data
- **Purpose:** To track your daily step count
- **Usage:** Step data is used to display your activity and calculate calorie credits
- **Control:** You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in your device settings

## AI Nutrition Analysis

When you use the meal analysis feature:
- Your meal description or photo is sent to a secure AI service for nutritional analysis
- This data is processed in real-time and is not stored by the AI service
- The nutritional results are returned to your device and stored locally

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Uninstalling the App will delete all your data
- You can clear app data through your device settings at any time

## Children's Privacy

SteadyPlate is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
